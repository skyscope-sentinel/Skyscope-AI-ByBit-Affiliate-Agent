## Bybit Platform Features (Summarized from Report)

**Core Trading Products:**
*   Spot Trading: Buying and selling cryptocurrencies at current market prices.
*   Derivatives Trading:
    *   USDT Perpetual Contracts
    *   Inverse Contracts
    *   Futures Contracts
    *   Options
*   Leverage Trading: Available for derivatives, allowing traders to open positions larger than their capital.
