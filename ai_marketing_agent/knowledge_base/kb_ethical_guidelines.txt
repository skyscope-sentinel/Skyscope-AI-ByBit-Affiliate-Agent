## Ethical Marketing and Compliance Guidelines (Summarized from Report Section 5)

**1. Disclosure of Affiliate Relationship:**
*   Mandatory: Clear and conspicuous disclosure (e.g., #Ad, #Sponsored, #BybitAffiliate, "Paid Partnership").
**2. Avoiding Misleading Claims and Guarantees:**
*   Truthful Advertising: All claims must be verifiable.
